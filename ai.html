What we work on

The outer layer

Which tools actually fit your work. How to set them up simply. How to build small daily habits that stick. No jargon. No overwhelm. If your grandmother can't use it, we simplify it until she can.

The inner layer

The parts of you that resist, freeze, procrastinate, or go into overdrive when something new lands. This is IFS applied to technology. It teaches you more about yourself than you'd expect.
